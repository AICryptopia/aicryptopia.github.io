Project Overview

The AI-Governed DAO Framework is a groundbreaking initiative that combines artificial intelligence with decentralized autonomous organizations to create more efficient, transparent, and automated governance systems. This framework aims to revolutionize how DAOs operate by implementing AI-driven decision-making processes while maintaining the core principles of decentralization and community governance.

Goals and Objectives

  • Develop AI models for proposal analysis and voting recommendations
  • Create automated treasury management systems
  • Implement predictive analytics for governance outcomes
  • Build reputation and contribution tracking mechanisms
  • Design scalable governance frameworks for different DAO types
