Project Overview

The AI-Powered DEX Analytics platform is designed to revolutionize how traders interact with decentralized exchanges by providing advanced analytics, predictive insights, and real-time market intelligence. By leveraging artificial intelligence and blockchain technology, we aim to create a comprehensive suite of tools that enhance trading decisions and market understanding.

Goals and Objectives

  • Develop a real-time analytics dashboard for DEX trading pairs
  • Implement AI-powered price prediction models for major trading pairs
  • Create pattern recognition algorithms for trading opportunities
  • Build a decentralized data collection and processing pipeline
  • Establish a token-based incentive system for contributors and users
